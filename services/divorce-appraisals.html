Divorce Appraisals in San Marcos, California

Dividing a marriage often means dividing a house, and that starts with an honest answer to what the property is worth. I provide independent appraisals of San Marcos homes for divorcing spouses and their family law attorneys, valuing the residence so it can be bought out, sold, or offset against other assets on fair footing.

A Neutral Number Both Sides Can Trust

The value of a divorce appraisal lies in its independence. I am not retained to favor the higher earner or the spouse staying in the home; I am retained to reach the figure the market supports. Because I have no stake in the outcome and take no direction on where the number should land, my conclusion holds up whether it is used in mediation, in settlement talks, or in front of a judge.

When both spouses agree to hire one appraiser together, I offer a reduced two-party fee. A single joint appraisal, priced below my standard $625 single-family fee, costs less than two competing reports, removes the argument over dueling numbers, and gives the court one clear valuation to work from. Either side remains free to ask me how I reached my conclusion.

Current or Retrospective Dates of Value

Family law matters do not always turn on today's value. Depending on your case, the court may want the home appraised as of the date of separation, the date of trial, or the present day. I can prepare current or retrospective valuations and will confirm the correct effective date with your attorney before I begin, since choosing the wrong date can undermine an otherwise sound report.

San Marcos Homes and the Details That Matter

A credible divorce appraisal has to reflect the specifics of the property, and San Marcos gives me plenty to weigh. View orientation toward Double Peak or the ridgelines, the size of the usable pad on a sloping lot, and the special-tax load in a master-planned tract like Rancho Tesoro all influence value. I document these features so that neither spouse can later argue the appraisal missed something obvious.
