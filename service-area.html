Where I Appraise in San Marcos

My focus is the City of San Marcos and the pockets of unincorporated County that sit within it, principally ZIP codes 92069 and 92078. San Marcos is far from uniform: a valuation has to account for master-planned hillside tracts, a lake community with age-restricted and all-age product, semi-rural valley acreage, and a student-rental submarket near two colleges. The neighborhoods below are the ones I work in regularly.

  • San Elijo Hills
  • Old Creek Ranch
  • Questhaven / La Costa Meadows (partly unincorporated)

Lake San Marcos & the Southwest 4 areas

  • Rancho Dorado
  • Coronado Hills
  • Stone Canyon

Discovery Hills & Central San Marcos 4 areas

North San Marcos & Twin Oaks Valley 6 areas

  • Santa Fe Hills
  • Richland
  • Richmar
  • Rock Springs / Bennett District
  • Rancho Santalina
  • Twin Oaks Valley (partly unincorporated)

Lake San Marcos is an unincorporated county island with a San Marcos mailing address; Twin Oaks Valley and the Questhaven corridor are split between the City of San Marcos and unincorporated San Diego County. Jurisdiction affects zoning, permitting, and taxing, and is verified for every assignment.
